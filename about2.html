What We Do

We are a team of designers, developers, and product enthusiasts on a mission to make Berkeley better with technology. Building whimsical, practical, and useful tech from the ground up - we will solve pressing problems, improve life on campus, and make students smile.



The New Member Experience

New members will join the club, and go through a crash course touching on design, technology, and product for the first few weeks. Get the hang of different design, tech, and product tools, and start to build something of your own.


Product

Learn how to brainstorm the best ideas, how to validate your ideas with user research, and how to successfully launch and manage your products.

Design

Understand the how and the why behind user-centric design, and mock up technology with Figma.

Technology

Dip your toes into different tech stacks and discover how to hack your way to an MVP on a time crunch.
